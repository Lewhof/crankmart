About This Event

The Cape Epic is Africa's leading three-day mountain bike stage race. Held annually in the Western Cape province, the Cape Epic attracts riders from across the globe. The event features challenging singletrack, stunning scenery, and a vibrant camping experience. Riders compete on a mixture of technical terrain and high-speed trails. The event is known for its organization, support, and inclusive atmosphere.
